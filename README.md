# Vrinda-Store-Sales-Dashboard-
 An interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze sales, orders, and customer trends for Vrinda Store. The dashboard uses PivotTables, PivotCharts, and Slicers to make the data fully interactive — filter by Month, Channel, or Category and watch every chart update instantly.

[Dashboard Preview](images/Vrinda Store.png)

🔍 Overview
This dashboard answers key business questions at a glance:

How are monthly orders and sales trending?
Which gender is driving more sales?
How healthy are our order fulfillment rates?
Which states and channels generate the most revenue?
How does order behavior differ by age group and gender?

🧰 Tools & Techniques Used
Microsoft Excel
PivotTables & PivotCharts
Slicers (Month, Channel, Category) for interactive filtering
Combo Chart (bar + line) for Order vs Sale trend
Pie/Doughnut Charts for categorical breakdowns
Clustered Bar/Column Charts for comparisons
Dashboard layout design (cards, consistent color theme, KPI grouping)

📈 Key Insights
Women are the primary customers, contributing 64% of total sales, while men contribute 36%.
92% of orders were successfully delivered, indicating strong order fulfillment performance.
Maharashtra generated the highest sales (₹2.99M), followed by Karnataka (₹2.65M) and Uttar Pradesh (₹2.10M).
Amazon is the top-performing sales channel, contributing 35% of total orders, followed by Myntra (23%) and Flipkart (22%).
Adult customers are the largest customer segment, with adult women generating the highest number of orders.
Sales peaked in February and gradually declined during the later months of the year, indicating possible seasonality in customer demand.
Cancelled, returned, and refunded orders account for only 8% of total orders, showing healthy operational performance.
Online marketplaces contribute the majority of sales, highlighting the importance of maintaining strong partnerships with e-commerce platforms.

🗂️ Dashboard Components
Order vs Sale – Monthly trend of total order count vs. sale amount
Sales: Men vs Women – Gender-wise sales contribution
Order Status – Delivered / Returned / Cancelled / Refunded breakdown
Sale: Top 5 States – Highest revenue-generating states
Order: Age vs Gender – Order percentage split across Adult, Senior, and Teenager segments by gender
Channels Order – Order share across Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Meesho, Ajio, Nykaa, and Others


🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Month — Jan to Dec
Channel — Ajio, Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra, etc.
Category — Blouse, Bottom, Ethnic, Kurta, Saree, Set, etc.

🚀 How to Use
Download Vrinda_Store_Dashboard.xlsx
Open in Microsoft Excel (Slicers require Excel 2010+)
Use the slicers on the left panel to filter by Month, Channel, or Category
All charts update dynamically based on your selection
